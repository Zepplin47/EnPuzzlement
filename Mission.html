Our Mission

Challenge Our Minds

Our goal is to overcome our own obstacles in life by help other overcome their own. Finding the time and resources to lift others up is our challenge. Making mental changes to the ideas of what service and charity can mean in Toledo.

Work as a Community

We our a family run organization. Communication and collaboration makes what we do possible. WIth each interaction we are building strength and possitivity in our community. 

Help the Less Fortunate

There comes a time when everyone goes under tough times or needs some support. We have been blessed with the good fortune of time and resources to help.
